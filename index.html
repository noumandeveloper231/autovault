Why dealers switch

The lot runs on gut feel and six open tabs.

Deals live in a spreadsheet, commissions in a text thread, expenses on a shoebox of receipts. By the time the numbers add up, the month is over. AutoVault closes the gap.

The spreadsheet stack
  • Numbers never match. Inventory, sales, and payments live in separate files that drift out of sync.
  • Commissions get argued. No single source of truth for who sold what and what they're owed.
  • Tax season is a scramble. Weeks spent rebuilding records your CPA can't read.
  • You find out too late. A thin-margin deal only shows up after the money's gone.

  • One connected ledger. Every vehicle, sale, and payment updates the same live P&L.

  • Export-ready for your CPA. Give your accountant secure access instead of a shoebox.
  • Margin in real time. Know the profit on a deal before you shake on it.
How it tracks every number

Every dollar tied to a VIN, a rep, or a filing.

Flip through the dashboard above and you'll see the same data broken down four ways. Here's what each screen is actually doing.

Cost to the penny, per vehicle

Purchase price, auction & doc fees, repairs, add-ons, flooring, and days on the lot roll into one true net-profit and ROI number — so you know which cars made money and which went underwater.

Sales tax kept on file

The tax you collect is captured on every sale and tied to the VIN, then organized by your filing frequency with the next due date front and center. No more rebuilding it at quarter-end.

Payroll & commissions in one run

Salespeople on base plus commission, mechanics and lot staff hourly, managers on salary — all calculated automatically, with every payout logged and proof kept on file.

A live profit & loss statement

Revenue minus cost of goods, commissions, and overhead — with gross and net margin — shows exactly where the money goes and where you're losing it, updated as deals close.

One platform, whole operation

Selling cars makes revenue. Tracking numbers builds wealth.

Increase profit

Cost, sale price, and margin on every vehicle — so a thin deal shows up before you shake on it, not after.

Save time

A sale updates inventory, transactions, payments, and commissions at once — no more re-typing the same deal four times.

Stay compliant

Every vehicle, payment, and expense logged with a paper trail your accountant and an auditor can both follow.

Access anywhere

Cloud-based, role-based access. Owner, sales manager, or CPA — everyone sees the same live numbers from any device.
